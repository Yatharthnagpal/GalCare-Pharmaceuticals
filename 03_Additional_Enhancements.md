# 03_Additional_Enhancements.md

# Galcare Website Enhancement Prompt

## Important Brand Requirement

**Do NOT change Galcare's existing brand identity.**

The existing **Green + White** color theme is an important part of the
company's identity and **must remain intact** throughout the redesigned
website.

Modernize the palette with improved shades, gradients, contrast, and
accessibility, but keep Green and White as the primary brand colors.

Secondary accent colors may include: - Dark Green - Light Green -
Emerald - Soft Gray - Charcoal (for text)

Avoid changing the overall visual identity to blue or any unrelated
pharmaceutical palette.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Overall Objective

Create a premium pharmaceutical website that feels modern, trustworthy,
and globally competitive while preserving Galcare's established
branding.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Homepage Improvements

Design a storytelling homepage with the following sections:

1.  Hero Banner
2.  About Galcare
3.  Three Core Divisions
    -   Neuropsychiatric
    -   Dermatology
    -   Third-Party Manufacturing
4.  Why Choose Galcare
5.  Manufacturing Excellence
6.  Research & Innovation
7.  Quality Assurance
8.  Certifications
9.  Featured Products
10. Statistics
11. Business Partners
12. Testimonials
13. Latest News
14. Contact CTA
15. Footer

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Dedicated Pages

Create detailed pages for: - About Us - Neuropsychiatric Division -
Dermatology Division - Third-Party Manufacturing - Manufacturing
Facilities - Research & Innovation - Quality Assurance - Product
Catalogue - Certifications - Careers - News & Media - Contact

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Reuse Existing Assets

Wherever possible:

-   Reuse images from the current Galcare website.
-   Preserve authentic manufacturing, laboratory, dermatology, and
    neuropsychiatric images.
-   Optimize images for performance (WebP/AVIF where appropriate).
-   Replace only low-quality assets that cannot be improved.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# UI & UX

Follow modern enterprise standards: - Consistent spacing (8pt grid) -
Rounded cards - Soft shadows - Premium typography - Sticky navigation -
Responsive layouts - Smooth scrolling - Accessible color contrast -
Elegant hover effects - Minimal, professional animations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Features

Include: - Searchable product catalogue - Third-party manufacturing
inquiry form - Download centre - Certification gallery - Manufacturing
process timeline - Interactive statistics - FAQ section - Careers
portal - Blog/News section

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# SEO & Performance

-   Semantic HTML
-   Core Web Vitals optimization
-   WCAG accessibility
-   Structured data
-   Sitemap
-   Robots.txt
-   Optimized metadata
-   Fast loading
-   Lazy loading

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Goal

The finished website should retain Galcare's recognizable Green & White
identity while delivering the premium user experience and
professionalism expected from a leading global pharmaceutical company.
The redesign should enhance---not replace---the brand.
